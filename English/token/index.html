    LRC Token

    Trend

    To learn more about LRC token, checkout https://etherscan.io/address/lrctoken.eth or https://etherscan.io/token/0xEF68e7C694F40c8202821eDF525dE3782458639f.

    Circulating Supply

    The current circulationg supply is 734,089,390 LRC, 52.6% of the total LRC supply.

    NOTE: There is a discrepancy regarding the circulationg supply between this document (734,089,390 LRC) and coinmarketcap.com (561,167,415 LRC as of Jan 17, 2018) . This is because we use different standards - The Loopring Foundation considers the LEAF fund as part of the circulationg supply although only a portion of it is actually in circulation.
